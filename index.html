How to Play & Mechanics

Rules of the Game:
1. Click cells to reveal them. If you hit a 💣, you lose (unless you have a shield).
2. Numbers show how many bombs are in the 8 surrounding cells.
3. Right-click to place a flag ⚑ on suspected bombs.
4. Clear all safe cells to win and progress to the next level!

  • Movement: Drag background to move. Mouse wheel or pinch to zoom.
  • Save / Load: Copy the code to save your level, score, and cooldowns!
  • Shields 🛡️: You get one every 5 levels. They automatically save you from one bomb!
  • Power-Ups: Use the sidebar on the right to scan areas, strike rows, or find safe paths when stuck.
