Help & Mechanics
  • Map Dragging & Zooming: Click and drag the background to move. Use your mouse wheel or pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out!
  • Bomb Counter: Shows total hidden bombs MINUS the flags you've placed.
  • Timer: Tracks seconds passed since your first click in the current level.
  • Click to Reset (Smiley): Instantly restart the current level anytime.
  • Save / Load: Generates a 1000-character secure code (Level, Score, Fails, Cooldowns). Copy to save, paste to resume!
  • Total Fails: Tracks your total mine explosions across all sessions.
  • Powerups (Right Sidebar): Use 🔍, 📡 and 🌟 to scan! Every 5 levels, you gain an automatic Shield 🛡️ to survive mine hits!
